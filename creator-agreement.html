1. Eligibility

  • You must be at least 18 years of age (or the age of majority in your jurisdiction, whichever is higher).
  • You must complete SHADOWNET’s identity and age verification process (government-issued photo ID plus live selfie) before publishing any content.
  • You must have the legal capacity to enter into this Agreement.
  • You must not be a sanctioned person or located in a sanctioned jurisdiction.

2. Prohibited Content and Activity

You agree that you will not upload, stream, or otherwise distribute any content that:

  • Depicts any minor (person under 18), including simulated, AI-generated, drawn, or animated depictions (CSAM).
  • Depicts non-consensual sexual activity, rape, trafficking, coercion, forced labor, or physical abuse.
  • Is illegal in any jurisdiction where it will be produced, uploaded, distributed, or viewed.
  • Violates any third-party intellectual property, privacy, or publicity right.
  • Promotes, glorifies, or facilitates human trafficking, exploitation, self-harm, or terrorism.
  • Is marketed using search terms, tags, titles, or descriptions that suggest CSAM or non-consensual content.

Any violation of this clause is grounds for immediate removal, account termination, forfeiture of pending payouts, and referral to law enforcement.

3. Written Consent of All Depicted Persons

Before uploading content that depicts any person other than yourself, you must obtain and retain, in writing, signed and dated consent from that person covering:

  • Consent to be depicted in the content.
  • Consent to public distribution of the content and to its upload to SHADOWNET.
  • Consent to download, where the content may be made downloadable to other users.

Consent records must be kept for the full duration of publication plus seven (7) years, and produced to SHADOWNET within five (5) business days of any request.

4. Identity and Age Verification of Depicted Persons

For every person (including yourself) depicted in any content you upload, you must:

  • Verify the identity and age of the person using a valid government-issued photo ID.
  • Confirm that the person is at least 18 years of age at the time of production.
  • Retain a copy of the identification and proof of verification.
  • Make such supporting documents available to SHADOWNET upon request within five (5) business days.

Failure to provide supporting documents results in immediate removal of the content and may result in account termination.

5. Pre-Screening and Moderation

You acknowledge that all uploaded content passes through SHADOWNET’s automated pre-screening pipeline (AI moderation, hash-matching, text/OCR scans) before becoming publicly visible. SHADOWNET may:

  • Delay publication pending manual review.
  • Refuse to publish or remove content that does not meet our Standards or applicable law.
  • Terminate live streams in real time on detected violations.

Any person depicted in content on SHADOWNET has an unconditional right to request the removal of that content, regardless of any earlier consent. SHADOWNET will remove the content within five (5) business days of receiving a verified request, unless you, as Content Provider, dispute the request.

If there is a dispute between you and a depicted person, and the dispute cannot be resolved by SHADOWNET’s internal review, it will be escalated to a neutral third-party arbitrator whose decision is final and binding on both parties, as required by the CCBill Acceptable Use Policy. While the arbitration is pending, the contested content remains offline.
