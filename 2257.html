18 U.S.C. § 2257 Compliance Statement

Last updated: April 23, 2026

English — In compliance with 18 U.S.C. § 2257 and 28 C.F.R. § 75, all models, actors, actresses and other persons appearing in any visual depiction of actual sexually explicit conduct hosted on SHADOWNET were over the age of eighteen (18) years at the time the depiction was created.

1. Scope

SHADOWNET is an online platform hosting user-generated content uploaded by independent creators. SHADOWNET does not produce, create, commission, or direct any of the content displayed on the platform. All content is produced by third-party creators who individually verify their identity and age via KYC prior to uploading any material.

2. Creator Verification (KYC)

Before any creator can upload monetizable or adult content, SHADOWNET requires:

  • Government-issued photo identification (passport, national ID card, or driver’s license)
  • A real-time selfie matching the submitted ID
  • Signed model release and content-ownership agreement
  • Explicit representation and warranty that the creator and any other person depicted is at least eighteen (18) years of age

Verification records are retained for a minimum of seven (7) years after account termination, consistent with 18 U.S.C. § 2257 record-keeping requirements.

4. Safe Harbor

SHADOWNET is an interactive computer service as defined in 47 U.S.C. § 230(f)(2) and a hosting service provider under the EU Digital Services Act (Regulation 2022/2065). SHADOWNET does not produce content and is not the publisher or speaker of any information provided by its users. All liability for content uploaded by creators lies with those creators. SHADOWNET qualifies for safe-harbor protections under both frameworks.

5. Prohibited Content

The following is strictly prohibited and will be removed immediately, the uploading account terminated, and, where applicable, reported to law enforcement (NCMEC, Belgian Federal Police, Interpol, local authorities):

  • Any content depicting or appearing to depict a minor (under 18)
  • Non-consensual content (revenge content, hidden cameras, leaked material)
  • Depictions of violence, non-consensual pain, rape, or trafficking
  • Bestiality, necrophilia, incest, or content involving animals
  • Content involving impaired consent (intoxication, sleep, unconsciousness)
  • Any content violating applicable law in Belgium, the European Union, or the United States
